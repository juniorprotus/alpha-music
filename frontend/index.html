Biography

The Visionary

Wax Kandle Alpha is a central figure in Nairobi’s vibrant Reggae and Dancehall scene, known for his signature blend of urban energy and conscious storytelling. Hailing from the heart of Nairobi, Kenya, Wax Kandle has carved out a unique space as an independent powerhouse, bridging the gap between street-level riddims and the classic roots of Reggae.

His career is defined by a deep commitment to collaboration and versatility. He has formed a formidable partnership with fellow artist Madstrit, resulting in a string of high-impact releases including 'Kumkum bhagya,' 'Addicted,' and 'Levels.' Solo works such as 'FOML (Fruits Of Mi Labour),' 'Tafuta,' and the soulful 'Pod Aheri' further showcase a lyrical depth that resonates with a growing global audience.

An artist who thrives on raw expression, Wax Kandle Alpha has built a reputation for his sharp, spontaneous freestyle ability and his talent for capturing authentic life through his visual content. Currently focused on expanding his reach from Nairobi to the international stage, he continues to push the boundaries of Kenyan music with every new single, staying true to his roots while embracing the future of the genre.
